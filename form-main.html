UNICEF: Join Us

UNICEF, an agency of the United Nations, is a global charity which aims to protect children's rights and well-being. The organization not only advocates for health, education, and nutrition services, but it also supports access to clean water, sanitation, and prevention of violence and abuse. Along with that, it also contributes to emergency aid during humanitarian emergencies, bringing life-saving help to those in need.
